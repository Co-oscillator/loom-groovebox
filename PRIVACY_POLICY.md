# Privacy Policy for Loom Groovebox

Loom Groovebox does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Audio Recording (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO)
The app requests access to the microphone solely for the **"Sampler"** feature, allowing users to record their own sounds for use within the application's groovebox engine. 
- **Processing**: Recorded audio is processed locally on the device.
- **Usage**: Audio is used only within the app for creative purposes (sampling).
- **Sharing**: Audio data is NOT uploaded to any servers or shared with any third parties.

## Files and Storage
The app accesses its own internal storage to save projects and recorded samples. It does not access unrelated personal files on the device.

## Data Collection
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics or crash-reporting tools that collect personal data.
- **No Tracking**: No behavioral tracking information is collected.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer.
